EXTRAIT DE L'ARTICLE [TGRK14] 60 D.1.3 Theory extensions of Integer O sets Our proof system also implements the schematic paramodulation calculus SUPC I to cope with theory extensions of Integer O sets. The following schematic saturations are computed by our proof system. Example 1 The schematic saturation of Ax(LLI)∪ G+ 0 consists of Ax(LLI), G+ 0 and the following schematic literals : s+(i1) = s+(i2)∥ const(i1, i2) i1̸= s+(i1)∥ const(i1, i2) s+(i1)̸= s+(i2)∥ const(i1, i2) s+(i1) = s+(len(a))∥ const(i1, a) len(a) = len(b)∥ const(a, b) s+(len(a)) = s+(len(b))∥ const(a, b) In the above schematic saturation, one can remark that s+(len(a)) = s+(len(b))∥ const(a, b) is obtained by a superposition of the constrained literal len(a) = s+(len(b))∥ const(a, b) with (a renaming of) itself.
