B.3.2 Run a computation Click on the "Load" button to choose an example  le, load a template, or upload your own Audacy input  le from your computer with the  le explorer. Then, choose a maximal expected duration (in seconds, minutes or hours) for the computation. Large durations have to be envisaged, because the prototype is not opti- mized and the server may be overloaded. Then, click on the "Launch audacy" button to transmit the input  le to the server. During the computation, the icon "loading.gif" appears on the button to indicate that the computation is running.
